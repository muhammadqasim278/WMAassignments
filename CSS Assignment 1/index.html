"Success"

In the journey of life, where dreams take flight,
There lies a quest for success, burning bright.
It's not just the laurels or treasures we amass,
But the lessons we learn while crossing the vast.

Success is not measured by mere worldly gains,
But by the battles fought amidst struggles and pains.
It's the courage to rise when we stumble and fall,
To heed the inner voice, to answer the call.

Success is the rhythm of a relentless beat,
A symphony composed by the dreams we meet.
It's the passion that ignites a fire within,
A relentless pursuit, where we always begin.

It's not the destination, but the path we tread,
The choices we make, and the risks we shed.
Success lies in the kindness we bestow,
The love we share, and the seeds we sow.

It's the laughter that echoes, the joy we find,
In moments of triumph, in ties that bind.
Success is the harmony of a life well lived,
With purpose and meaning, our souls are uplifted.
