Haleem 1 kg refers to the quantity of haleem that weighs approximately 1 kilogram. It is a larger serving size typically used for catering or when serving a larger group of people. Haleem is a slow-cooked stew made with a combination of meat (often beef or mutton), lentils, and grains, such as wheat or barley. It is a rich and flavorful dish that is popular in South Asian and Middle Eastern cuisine.
