Beef Yakhni Pulao is a traditional South Asian rice dish made with beef, fragrant spices, and aromatic broth known as yakhni. When referred to as "regular," it signifies the standard or typical preparation of the dish without any specific variations. In Beef Yakhni Pulao, beef is cooked in a flavorful broth made by simmering meat, spices, and herbs. This yakhni broth is then used to cook the rice, resulting in a delicious and aromatic rice dish with tender beef pieces. The spices commonly used in Yakhni Pulao include cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. Onions, garlic, ginger, and other flavoring ingredients may also be added.
